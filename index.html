About SISA

Our story and vision

Our Mission

Founded in 2025, SISA is the vibrant heart of the Indian student community at the University of Siegen. We're a passionate group dedicated to creating a home away from home for students from India.

Our mission is to bridge cultures, provide academic support, and create memorable experiences that enrich your student life in Germany.

Established in 2025

With 25 founding members and a vision for cultural connection

Our Vision

We're building the future of Indian student life in Siegen with these core goals:

Industry Partnerships

Connecting students with leading companies for internships and career opportunities

Cultural Exchange

Promoting Indian culture while embracing German traditions through collaborative events

Academic Excellence

Supporting student success through mentorship programs and study groups

Community Building

Creating a supportive network for Indian students in Siegen

Cultural Center

Establishing a dedicated space for Indian cultural activities

Support System

Providing comprehensive assistance for new students arriving in Germany
