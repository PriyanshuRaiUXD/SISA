ABOUT SISA.

SISA (Siegen Indian Students Association) is a nonprofit organization supporting the Indian community in Siegen, Germany. We foster cultural exchange, provide practical assistance, and build a social network through events, workshops, and peer mentoring.

Our Vision

Cultural Events

From vibrant Diwali nights to colorful Holi celebrations, we bring the spirit of Indian festivals to Siegen.

Career Networking

We connect students and professionals with industry leaders through workshops and networking events.

Integration Support

We provide practical support, from language tandems to informational sessions on life in Germany.

OUR HISTORY

  • FEBRUARY 2025
    The Idea is Born

    A small group of students at the University of Siegen gather, sharing a desire to create a stronger sense of community and a platform to celebrate shared culture far from home.

  • APRIL 2025
    First Community Event

    SISA organizes its first event, a vibrant Holi celebration at a local park. The overwhelming turnout highlights the need for a formal association.

  • JUNE 2025
    Official Founding

    With a clear mission and growing support, SISA is officially established, forming its first student board and advisory panel to serve the Indian community in Siegen.

Embassy Assistance

Need Guidance with Official Matters?

While SISA is not an official government body, we can help guide you to the right resources for embassy-related queries like passports, visas, or OCI cards. If you need help understanding a process, open a request, and a board member will try to assist.
